Why this exists

I was never rewarded for being good, because I never manipulated. I was noticed — and the people who noticed chose to take advantage of me. It took me most of my life to stop thinking this was a personal problem and start seeing it as the architecture: a society that rewards masks and punishes the real.

I am not building an audience. I am assembling a group that has never existed in history — the first organized formation of people willing to lose what they are to become what they actually are. There have always been the one‑offs. There has never been the group. I don’t know what happens when there is. I am going to find out.

If this is landing against something already inside you, this was for you.
